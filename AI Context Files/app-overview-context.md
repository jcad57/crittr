### Crittr

Crittr is an app that allows pet owners to care for their pets on their own or with a co-carer. Crittr let's users add pets to their profile, log activities, meals and health related information. On top of that users will be able to export their data in .pdf format which they can share with other people of interest like their vet. The exported data will include activity patterns logged in the app, food information and medications. This will allow pet owners to house all data related to their pets in one place, making it easy to manage health concerns, activity requirements and meals as well as share this with others of interest like vets and people who they may board their pet with.

# Dashboard

The dashboard allows users to get a general overview of information for their pets. They can switch the active pet to see details for the active pet. They will see a circular progress bar for main metrics like exercise, meals, treats and medications. As they log activities the progress bar will change until they've completed them for the day.

The activity section will show the logged activities for the day. From here they can manage those activities or add new ones. They can navigate to the activity page which will give more comprehensive details for each pet and show data for all days. They can filter these activities by type/date/pet.

The health section will give a general overview of any medications pets are currently on and allow the user to log administrations of these medications so the user and other co-carers will know if medications are needed for that day.
