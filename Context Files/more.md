## This document outlines the additional pages that will be available to users on the 'more' tab in the navigator

# Learn

User will be able to read up on ways to best care for their pet. It will have individualized articles, links and information for their pet type.

# CrittrAI (Subscription)

AI assistant chat that will let the user ask questions about caring for their pet using AI assistance. It will give well researched information and answer general questions but always caution the owner to consult a vet professional
